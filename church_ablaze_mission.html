Who We Are

A Church
Set on Fire

Church Ablaze Mission is a Spirit-filled community of believers united by one confession — Jesus Is Lord. We are a multi-branch family serving our communities across the region with love, prayer, and the Word of God.

"And they were all filled with the Holy Spirit and began to speak with other tongues, as the Spirit gave them utterance."

Acts 2:4

PM

General PM Mathebula & Mrs Bishop Mathebula

Founders & Overseers — Church Ablaze Mission

Under the visionary leadership of General PM Mathebula and his wife, Church Ablaze Mission was born out of a deep calling to see souls saved and believers filled with the Holy Spirit. Based at Clare Church Ablaze Mission, their ministry continues to expand the kingdom of God across our nation and beyond.
